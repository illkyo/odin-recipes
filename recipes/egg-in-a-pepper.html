Egg in a Pepper

Description

This egg in a pepper recipe is paleo and a super easy way to eat your eggs! You can also top it with cheese or even a slice of bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 (1/4-inch thick) ring bell pepper
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat
  2. Add bell pepper ring
  3. Crack egg into bell pepper ring; cook until bottom holds together and corners are browned, 2 to 3 minutes
  4. Flip; cook until desired doneness is reached, 2 to 3 minutes more
  5. Season with salt and ground black pepper
